Key Findings & Business Insights

Launch Site & Orbit Trends

Orbit Success Rate: Low energy orbits (LEO, ISS, SSO) show 100% success. High GTO orbits show ~50% success rates, due to landing fuel constraints.

Yearly Landing Success: Success rate rose from 0% in 2013 to over 80% in 2020, representing the maturation of reusable rocket recoveries.

Strategic Bidding & Underwriting Recommendation

  • Competitor Strategy: Price commercial bids dynamically according to booster landing probabilities ($40M-$50M hardware savings).
  • Insurance Strategy: Adjust underwriting premium scales between low risk LEO missions and high delta-v GTO transfers.
