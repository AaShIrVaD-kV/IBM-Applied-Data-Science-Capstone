Project Overview & Business Value

SpaceX recovers and refurbishes the first-stage Falcon 9 booster core to save over 60% of production costs. This recovery capability is a major disruption in the space transportation industry, letting SpaceX price launches at ~$62M, compared to competitors' expendable launches costing ~$165M+.

The Objective: Build a classification model to determine whether a rocket's first stage will land successfully on ground pads or autonomous spaceport drone ships, aiding competitors in bidding strategies and insurers in calculating risk.
